Quiz Game Rules

Read these rules carefully before playing

Game Objective

  • Complete all 5 levels by answering questions correctly
  • Each level has 15 questions, but only 10 random questions are shown
  • Earn stars and points based on your performance
  • Questions cover web development topics from basic to advanced

Scoring System

  • Correct answer: +10 points
  • Wrong answer: 0 points (no penalty)
  • 3 Stars: 80% or higher (8+ correct answers)
  • 2 Stars: 60-79% (6-7 correct answers)
  • 1 Star: 40-59% (4-5 correct answers)
  • 0 Stars: Below 40% (less than 4 correct)

Level Progression

  • Level 1 is unlocked by default for all players
  • You must earn at least 1 star to unlock the next level
  • Complete Level 1 → Unlock Level 2, and so on
  • You can replay completed levels to improve your score
  • Higher levels have more difficult questions

Timer

  • The timer tracks how long you take to complete the level
  • There is no time limit - take as long as you need
  • Your completion time is shown in the game header
  • Challenge yourself to answer quickly and accurately!

Leaderboard

  • Your best score for each level is saved automatically
  • Leaderboard ranks players by total stars earned
  • If stars are equal, total score determines ranking
  • Compete with yourself and improve your rankings!

Tips for Success

  • Read each question carefully before answering
  • Take your time - there's no time pressure
  • Review the correct answer when you get it wrong
  • Practice makes perfect - replay levels to improve!
